❓ Help Center & FAQ

How does FocusEye calculate attention?

It processes focus, tracking eye gaze direction, head orientation, and facial emotion metrics to build an attention percentage.

Why is the camera in "Simulation Mode"?

If camera access is blocked or missing on the host computer, it defaults to simulation mode to continue rendering analytical cards.
